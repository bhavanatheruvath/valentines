Rose Day

Rose Day is the first day of Valentine's Week, celebrated on February 7th. It's a day to express your love and affection by gifting roses to your loved ones. Different colors of roses symbolize different emotions, so choose the right color to convey your feelings.

Red roses are traditionally associated with love and passion, making them perfect for romantic partners. Pink roses symbolize admiration and gratitude, while yellow roses represent friendship and joy. White roses are often associated with purity and innocence.

Task: Buy a beautiful red rose for your loved one.
